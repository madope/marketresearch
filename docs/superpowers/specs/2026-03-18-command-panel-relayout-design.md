# Command Panel Relayout Design

## Goal

Reduce vertical whitespace above the workflow area by moving the research input form out of the top hero area and into the first row of content cards.

## Layout

- Keep the top hero area as a compact title and description only.
- Remove the existing "价格概览" card.
- Place the research prompt form and action buttons in that card position.
- Keep "任务进度" in the left card of the first row.

## Expected Outcome

- The workflow panel sits much closer to the prompt form.
- The page no longer has a large empty form area at the top.
- The primary interaction area and progress area are visible together without extra whitespace.
